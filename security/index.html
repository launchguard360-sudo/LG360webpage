Security

How LaunchGuard360™ ensures encryption, governance, and access controls across all sustainment processes.

Last updated: December 2024

Encryption Standards

All data is encrypted in-transit and at-rest using industry-standard protocols (TLS 1.2+ for transit, AES-256 for storage). Encryption keys are managed with strict lifecycle policies.

Governance & Access Controls

  • Role-based access controls ensure only authorized staff can access sensitive systems.
  • All access is logged and reviewed regularly for compliance.
  • Separation of duties enforced for critical operations.

Infrastructure Security

  • Systems hosted in secure, compliant data centers (ISO 27001, SOC 2).
  • Redundancy and failover across multiple availability zones.
  • Regular penetration testing and vulnerability scanning.

Incident Response

We maintain a documented incident response plan including:

  • 24/7 monitoring of systems for anomalies
  • Clear escalation procedures and communication protocols
  • Post-incident reviews to strengthen controls

Client Controls

Clients remain in control of their governance data. LaunchGuard360™ does not share client insights externally without explicit consent.
